Menene Delta?

Delta shiri ne na tsarin kuɗin dijital na duniya wanda ke buƙatar ci gaba na dogon lokaci, da kuma tsarin yanayin aikace-aikacen kuɗi.

Menene alkiblar Delta?

'Yan Adam za su iya amfani da Delta don gina haɗin gwiwa na duniya, gaskiya, amintaccen, sauri, da sabon kudin dijital kore. Kuma samar da yanayi daban-daban masu tasiri na aikace-aikacen don waɗannan kuɗaɗen dijital da agogon crypto na baya.

Wanene ƙungiyar ci gaban Delta?

Haida mai shirye-shirye ne ya jagoranci farkon ci gaban Delta. Daga baya, duk wanda ya yarda da tsarin ci gaban Delta da shirin ci gaba kuma yana da hazaka masu dacewa zai sami damar zama cikin ƙungiyar masu haɓaka Delta.

Wace fasaha Delta ke amfani da ita?

Jigon tsarin Delta yana gudana akan dandamalin kwangilar kwamfuta mai wayo ta Intanet kuma ana aiwatar da shi cikin yaren motoko. An haɓaka kuma ana aiwatar da ƙa'idar wayar hannu ta gaba a cikin Flutter.

Me talaka zai iya yi a Delta Kim?

Kowane mutum na iya bayar da gudummawa ga ci gaban yarjejeniya na Delta.

  • 1. Ma'adinai
  • 2. Ba da shawarar wasu a matsayin jakada
  • 3. Zama mai inganci kuma ba da sabis na tashar tabbatarwa
  • 4. Shiga USTD dual-currency mining
  • 5. Haɓaka man fetur na ICP
  • 6. Samar da shawarwarin ci gaba da jefa kuri'a a sashin taswirar hanya
  • 7. Shiga cikin gine-gine da ma'amaloli daban-daban na muhalli
  • ...

Yadda ake fahimtar hakar ma'adinan Delta?

Mahimmancin hakar ma'adinai a Delta shine tsarin rarrabawa, wanda ba shi da alaƙa da abubuwa kamar ikon sarrafa kayan aiki da bandwidth na cibiyar sadarwa.

Menene bambanci tsakanin DTC da DTCT?

DTC ita ce gajarta ta Delta Coin, DTCT ita ce gajarta ta Delta Credit, kuma DTCT ita ce DTC wacce ba a kunna ba kuma ta fito.

Menene jimillar adadin DTC?

An tsara DTC don samun jimlar adadin biliyan 300 a farkon matakin. A cewar farar takarda, bayan kafa "Kwamitin Mulki", za ta kada kuri'a don yanke shawarar ko za a sake fitar da kuma shirin fitar da shi.

Yadda ake samun ƙarin DTC?

  • 1. Ci gaba da hakar ma'adinai
  • 2. Zama jakada da ba da shawarar wasu
  • 3. Zama mai inganci kuma ba da sabis na tashar tabbatarwa
  • 4. Tada kudi don man ICP
  • 5. Shiga cikin ginin muhalli don samun ladan ginin muhalli

Menene jakadan gabatarwa? Yaya ake lissafin ladan ƙungiyar?

Lokacin da kuka gayyaci wasu don zama membobin Delta ta hanyar DID ɗin ku, zaku zama jakadan talla ta atomatik.

Duk lokacin da kuka ba da shawarar mutum ɗaya kai tsaye, ƙimar ma'adinan ku zai ƙaru da 1/3 na ƙimar asali; duk lokacin da kuka ba da shawarar a kaikaice (shawarwari na biyu) mutum ɗaya, ƙimar ma'adinai zai ƙaru da 1/10 na ƙimar asali.

Menene 3-Ba Tabbaci ba?

Yana nufin cewa masu amfani basa buƙatar maɓalli na sirri, mnemonic, ko kalmar sirri don shigar da tsarin. Hakanan ana kiransa tantancewar SMS da aka raba.

Shin ina buƙatar sarrafa maɓallan sirri, mnemonics ko kalmomin shiga lokacin amfani da Delta?

A'a. Ba kwa buƙatar sarrafa maɓallan sirri, jimloli ko kalmomin sirri saboda Delta tana aiki akan ka'idar 3-Babu Tabbatarwa.

Menene da'irar tsaro?

Da'irar tsaro samfuri ne na tabbatarwa dangane da alaƙar zamantakewar mai amfani, kuma wannan aikin an karkasa shi gaba ɗaya. Masu amfani suna ƙara amintattun mutane 3-5 a cikin da'irar alaƙar zamantakewa don samar da da'irar tsaro, da taimaka musu cimma tabbacin tsaro na biyu idan ya cancanta. Ayyukan da'irar tsaro na iya haɓaka wahalar hare-haren ƙeta yadda ya kamata da rage haɗarin samun wasu asusu.

Menene ma'adinan kuɗaɗe biyu na USDT?

USDT ma'adinai biyu-kudi wani sashe ne daban da aka tanada baya ga hakar ma'adinan DTC. Masu amfani za su iya samun DTCT ta ziyartar shafin USCT Mining akan Delta app da kallon tallace-tallacen da abokan tallanmu ke bayarwa ko kuma kammala ayyukan da abokan aikinmu suka bayar.

Menene darajar DTC?

An ƙaddara ƙimar DTC ta hanyar amincewar duk membobin Delta, kuma farashin ƙarshe yana nunawa ta hanyar ciniki tsakanin mai siye da mai siyar da kayan. Don haka, babu wanda zai iya saita farashi don DTC.

Yaushe DTC zai sami darajar? Yaushe zan iya juya DTC zuwa "kudi na gaske"?

Bayan mai amfani ya wuce KYC, a hankali za a sake shi bisa ga yarjejeniyar farar takarda, kuma DTC da aka saki za ta iya yawo cikin yardar kaina.

Zan iya shigar Delta app akan na'urori da yawa a lokaci guda don hakar ma'adinai?

A'a, kowa zai iya riƙe asusun Delta ɗaya kawai don hakar ma'adinai, kuma membobin da suke ma'adinai akai-akai za a cire su kuma a hukunta su yayin matakin KYC.

Menene Wallet Multi-Chain Delta?

Wallet ɗin sarkar sarka mai yawa yana ɗaya daga cikin mahimman ayyukan da aka gina a cikin tsarin Delta. Alamar sarkar sarkar Delta da yawa tana bawa masu amfani damar canja wurin kadarori, musayar bayanai da haɗin gwiwa tare da sauran blockchain. Yana iya karɓa da aika wasu kadarorin sarkar jama'a cikin sauƙi, kuma lokacin da masu amfani ke sarrafa waɗannan kadarorin, ba sa buƙatar kowane maɓalli na sirri, abubuwan tunawa ko kalmomin shiga. Wannan yanayin dacewa kuma amintaccen fasalin ya samo asali ne saboda ƙa'idar tabbatarwa-babu uku na Delta da sa hannu na kofa na ECDSA na tushen Kwamfuta (Sa hannu na ECDSA na Kofin).

Menene ICP crowdfunding?

Hanya ce don Delta don haɓaka mai don aikin kwangila mai wayo. Masu amfani za su iya tayar da alamun man fetur na ICP kuma su sami takamaiman adadin DTC/DTCT.

Menene dApp Square?

Dandalin DApp shine kasuwar aikace-aikacen da aka raba ta Delta, dandali don gina muhallin kan layi na Delta, da cibiyar aikace-aikacen Web3 mai gaba-gaba. Duk wani mai amfani da ikon ci gaba zai iya haɓaka aikace-aikacen Delta kuma ya zauna a ciki.

Delta aikin budaddi ne?

Delta ta buɗe wasu ƙananan ayyuka kuma sannu a hankali za ta buɗe tushen su kamar yadda aka tsara.
